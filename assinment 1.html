My Favorite Hobby

Yoga and Reading Books

Yoga is my favorite way to stay healthy and relaxed.
I practice yoga every morning. It helps me stay focused, calm, and gives energy for the whole day.
Yoga improves not only my physical health but also gives peace to my mind.

Reading books is something I truly enjoy.
Books open my imagination and help me explore new ideas. I mostly read motivational and self-improvement books.
This habit keeps me inspired and makes me feel productive every day.

Biography of Albert Einstein

About Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was born in Germany in 1879.
Since childhood, he was very curious and interested in maths and physics. He had a deep love for learning.

Achievements

He is most famous for his theory of relativity and the formula E = mc².
Einstein was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his work on the photoelectric effect.

Inspiration

He proved that creativity and intelligence go hand in hand.
Einstein was also a peace lover and believed in using knowledge for the good of humanity.

My Daily Routine

I start my day at 6:30 AM. After waking up, I do some yoga and light exercise to feel fresh and active. Then I go for a short walk to relax my mind.

After getting refreshed, I attend my online or college classes. I listen carefully and try to complete my assignments on time. In the afternoon, I revise my lessons and study the topics that need more focus. I also take out time to read books — mostly motivational or knowledge-based.

In the evening, I go for a walk again to stay healthy. Later, I spend most of my time in coding and learning new programming skills. I try to solve problems, build projects, and improve my logic every day. Finally, I sleep on time so that I stay fresh and energetic the next day.
